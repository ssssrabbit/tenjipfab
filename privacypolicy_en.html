1. Collection of Information

"Tenji P-Fab" (hereinafter referred to as "this App") does not collect or store any user personal information, usage data, device IDs, or location data. Any text data entered into the App is processed temporarily in the device's memory for Braille conversion purposes only and is never permanently saved or transmitted.

2. Data Transmission and Communication

This App does not possess any functionality to send data to external servers or communicate via the Internet. All processes, including Japanese language analysis and Braille data generation, are completed entirely within your device (locally). Therefore, your input data will never be leaked externally.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

Since we do not collect any user information, we do not share or provide data to any third parties. Furthermore, we do not incorporate any information collection modules, such as advertising SDKs or user behavior analysis tools.
